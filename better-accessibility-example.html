Banana and oat smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 small Banana
  • ¼ cup Dry rolled oats
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ tsp Ground cinnamon

Instructions

  • Blend banana, rolled oats, oat milk and a pinch of cinnamon until smooth and frothy.
